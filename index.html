Biography

ADAM LOCASCIO

Based in Boston, Massachusetts, Adam Locascio is an award-winning attorney who served as a captain and judge advocate with the United States Marine Corps from 2013 to 2018. In this role, Adam Locascio was awarded the Legal Assistance Military Personnel Award from the American Bar Association.

While serving in the Marine Corps, Capt Locascio was the officer-in-charge of the Marine Corps Air Ground Combat Center Tax Center. He also provided legal assistance to service members, conducted legal compliance reviews, and worked on special research projects within the Judge Advocate Division. More recently, he served as an attorney-advisor with the Social Security Administration.

Adam Locascio holds memberships with the Bar Association of DC. He is highly knowledgeable about litigation, consumer law, and performing legal research and has extensive legal expertise in governance, criminal justice, and technology.
